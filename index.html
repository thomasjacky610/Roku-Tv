How to Connect Roku to Your TV

Follow this simple step-by-step guide to set up your Roku device and start streaming in minutes.

Step 1 — Plug Roku into HDMI Port

Insert your Roku device into an available HDMI port on your TV. Make sure the connection is secure.

Step 2 — Connect Power Cable

Use the provided USB power cable and adapter to power your Roku device. Turn on your TV.

Step 3 — Select the Correct HDMI Input

Using your TV remote, switch the input source to the HDMI port where Roku is connected.

Step 4 — Pair the Roku Remote

Insert batteries into the remote. Follow the on-screen instructions to pair it with your Roku device.

Step 5 — Connect to Wi-Fi

Select your Wi-Fi network and enter the password. A stable internet connection is required for streaming.

Tip: Keep your router close to the TV for a stronger signal.

Step 6 — Activate Your Roku

Follow the on-screen steps to activate your Roku device. You may need to create or sign in to a Roku account.

Step 7 — Start Streaming

Once setup is complete, you can install channels and begin streaming your favorite content.
